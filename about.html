Who We Are

HallieForAnimals is a grassroots movement dedicated to exposing animal cruelty, advocating for stronger laws, and empowering the public to take action.

We believe that awareness drives accountability—and accountability saves lives.

Every investigation, every post, and every call-to-action we share aims to shine a light on injustice and pressure authorities to act. Our work bridges the gap between online outrage and real-world impact.

Our Mission

To ensure no act of cruelty goes unnoticed or unpunished. We track ongoing abuse cases, collect verifiable reports, and mobilize public support through targeted campaigns that reach prosecutors, lawmakers, and media outlets.

We don’t rely on shock value—we rely on truth, evidence, and persistence.

Our Approach

  • Identify and verify animal-cruelty cases shared online
  • Research local jurisdictions and points of contact
  • Create email and phone campaigns to demand accountability
  • Publish updates as cases progress or conclude

Everything we do is transparent, documented, and focused on outcomes—not outrage.

Get Involved

Whether you’re reporting cruelty, helping gather evidence, or simply sharing our posts, your involvement matters. Together, we turn empathy into action.
